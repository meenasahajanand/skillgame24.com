Each character you style is presented with realistic features, and their expressions and movements bring a touch of life to every scene. The designs come together to make you feel like you’re truly in a high-end fashion world, where your artistic choices are on full display.

Playing Style My Day: Makeup Tales is simple yet deeply engaging. Your role as a stylist is to create flawless makeup looks and outfits that perfectly suit the character and their current situation. The game’s structure revolves around different events and photoshoots, each with its own storyline and aesthetic goals. You’ll meet various models, each with their unique backstory and preferences, and it’s up to you to make styling decisions that reflect their personality and the mood of the event.

As you progress, more items and tools become available, allowing you to get more creative with each look.
